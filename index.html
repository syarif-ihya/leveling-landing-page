[ System Features ]

Designed to Level You Up

🤖
AI Quest Engine

Every quest is generated by AI specifically for your profile, your current skills, and your stated goal. No templates. No filler.

🔀
Branch Quest System

Complete a quest and the System analyzes what you built. Successor, Exploration, or Mastery — your next path adapts to what you actually did.

📊
Real Attribute Growth

XP distributes across 8 attributes based on the nature of your work. A coding quest builds INT. A public speaking quest builds CHA. Growth is specific.

🧠
Honest Rank System

Your rank is earned, not given. The System won't flatter you. Re-evaluation is triggered by real completed quests — not by asking nicely.

💀
Failure Is Data

Failed quests generate recovery branches. The System analyzes why you failed — scope, motivation, or capability — and calibrates the next attempt accordingly.

🪙
Resource Collection

Real-world outputs — deployed projects, certificates, new connections — become Resources in your profile. Your privileges grow as you complete quests.
