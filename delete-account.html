Account Deletion – Leveling App

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data in the Leveling App, please follow these steps in the app:

  • Open the Leveling App
  • Go to Settings
  • Navigate to the Account section
  • Tap the Delete Account button
  • Follow the instructions to confirm the deletion

Data That Will Be Deleted

Once confirmed, our system will immediately and permanently delete the following data:

  • User account data (including email and user ID)
  • Progress data such as quests, XP, skills, and items

We do not retain your data after the deletion process is complete. All data is permanently removed from our systems (Firestore and Firebase Authentication) and cannot be recovered.
